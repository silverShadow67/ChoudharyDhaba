About Choudhary Dhaba

Established as a classic roadside dhaba, Choudhary Dhaba brings the true essence of Punjabi and North Indian cuisine to Jaipur. With our open tandoor, fresh ingredients, and traditional recipes passed down generations, we serve hearty meals in a warm, rustic atmosphere perfect for families, friends, and travelers.
